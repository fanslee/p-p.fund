Decentralized Charity

Peace Panda: A Participant in Decentralized Charity We are participating in a decentralized charitable revolution. As a participant in Decharity (Decentralized Charity), a blockchain-based decentralized charity organization, Peace Panda is committed to global peace and philanthropy, advocating for blockchain users to work together to protect the rights of ordinary people affected by war.

Peace Panda utilizes the FDME (Free Mint Equity) model. Through fair minting, it eliminates the insider trading risks of traditional meme coins, ensuring fair participation for all participants and truly realizing a transparent and open community model.
